Cafe Purr

Café Purr is your local haven where quality drinks meet adorable feline company. Established with the goal of creating a relaxing, cat-friendly atmosphere, we invite guests to relax, enjoy and connect with both coffee and cats.

Our Mission

At Café Purr, our mission is to create a warm, inclusive space where people can unwind, connect, and experience the simple joys of great coffee and compassionate companionship. By blending a cozy café environment with the therapeutic presence of cats, we aim to support animal welfare, and bring moments of peace and joy to every guest.

Our Vision

We envision a community where people and animals thrive together. Café Purr strives to be more than just a coffee shop, its a space that fosters connection, encourages kindness, and advocates for the wellbeing of animals. Through partnerships and a focus on mindful hospitality, we dream of a future where every cat has a home and every guest leave with a smile.

Our Coffee

All coffee is freshly brewed upon order using premium beans. We take pride in delivering rich and satisfying flavors in every cup.

Our Food

Our pastries and snacks are baked fresh and made with top quality ingredients. Whether you are dining in or ordering to go, we ensure that every bite is delicious and safe.

Our Cats

Our cats are well cared for, fully vaccinated, and trained to interact gently with customers. They are playful and clean and ready to make your visit extra special

Visiting Guidelines

  1. Children under the age of 12 must be supervised at all times.
  2. Guests with cat allergies are advised to proceed with caution
  3. Be gentle when interacting with the cats.
  4. No flash photography when taking photos of the cats.
  5. Food waste and leftovers are strictly prohibited.
  6. Please maintain respectful and avoid disturbing other guests.
